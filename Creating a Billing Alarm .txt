Creating a Billing Alarm 

Login to AWS Console and Click your name and click 'My Billing Dashboard'

- you can see costs occurred until now!
- Click 'Preferences' and can select 
  Receive PDF Invoice By Email
  Receive Billing Alerts - Click this (you can revert this setting)
  Receive Billing Reports
  
  and Click 'Manage Billing Alerts'
  Click Alarms and Create Alarm
  Browse and Select Metric
  and Define Alarm
  then at last Create Alarm
  it can send email to with all alarming level of util/money and so on
  per instance level we can do that
